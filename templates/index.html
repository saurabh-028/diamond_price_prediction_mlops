About Dataset

Context: These dataset contain 10 features in which 'Price(in US dollars)' is the dependent feature. The goal is to predict the price of Diamond using different Regression Algorithms.

Acknowledgements: Attributes Information

  • Carat (Weight of Diamond): Weight of Diamond.
  • Cut (Quality): Quality of cut (Fair, Good, Very Good, Premium, Ideal).
  • Color: Diamond Color (from J -> 'worst' to D -> 'Best').
  • Clarity: Measurement of Transparency (how clear the Diamond is). Sequence of clarity (I1 (worst quality), SI2, SI1, VS2, VS1, VVS2, VVS1, IF (best quality)).
  • Table: Width of top of a Diamond.
  • Price (in US dollars): Price of Diamond in US dollars.
  • X (length): Length of Diamond in mm.
  • Y (width): Width of Diamond in mm.
  • Z (depth): Depth of Diamond in mm.
  • Depth: Total depth percentage. It can be calculated by a simple formula: Total Depth % = Z / Mean(X, Y) or Z * 2 / (X + Y).
